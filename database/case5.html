OPENING ACADEMY BATCH 26-4 OFFICIALLY OPENS

Monday, May 18, 2026
Community Liaison Office

Los Santos - On May 18, 2026, the Los Santos Fire Department officially opened the Opening Academy Batch 26-4 activities which were held at Fire Station 82, Market, Los Santos.

In this academy opening, 6 participants were officially accepted to participate in the academy training program as part of the formation and development process of new Los Santos Fire Department personnel.

The academy program is scheduled to run from May 19, 2026 to May 30, 2026. During the training period, participants will receive various basic materials such as Basic Life Support (BLS) Kit, use of radio communication, and basic fire extinguishing training as part of the formation of firefighter operational capabilities.

The Los Santos Fire Department hopes that all academy participants can follow each training process well and demonstrate high dedication in preparing themselves to become professional firefighters and ready to serve the people of Los Santos City.
